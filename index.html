I design and develop mixed-initiative creative interfaces that use artificial intelligence to support human creativity, particularly in the domains of storytelling, poetry, and game design. My research aims to offload the painful and tedious parts of creative work onto the computer, empowering diverse users to create expressive artifacts that push the boundaries of human expressive potential.
