Epitaph

A browser-based, text-only idlegame about existential risks and the death of civilizations, made for the Fermi Paradox Jam in September 2016. Interfere with the development of procedurally generated alien civilizations and try to keep them from going extinct, even at the risk of hastening them to their doom.

TracerySharp

A port of Kate Compton’s Tracery project – a language, library, and tool for procedural text generation – to C# and Unity. Compatible with existing Tracery grammars, so you can (for instance) take a Cheap Bots Done Quick bot and drop its grammar straight into your Unity game to generate NPC dialogue.

Stepwise

An experiment in automatically generating step-by-step explanations of the evaluation process for different pieces of code. Potentially useful as an assistive tool for learning and reasoning about code execution. Inspired partly by Bret Victor’s essay on Learnable Programming, and partly by explainshell.com.
