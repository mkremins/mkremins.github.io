--- title: About me, about this site ---

Hi there! I’m Max Kreminski. I’m currently a PhD student in Computational Media at the Expressive Intelligence Studio at UC Santa Cruz. I frequently find myself designing games; programming computers; writing words; and conducting research on the subjects of human-computer interaction, creativity, and play.

Affording Play is my blog. Frequently discussed topics include:

  • Creativity-enabling tools, especially casual creators.
  • Mixed-initiative computational creativity: teaching computers to work alongside humans as creative collaborators and not just tools.
  • Games, interactive storytelling, and play.
  • UX design, UI design, and user research.
  • Anything else that happens to be on my mind.

You should follow me on Twitter! If you want, you can also email me; my address is just Max Kreminski (minus the space) at Gmail.
