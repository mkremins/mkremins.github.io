--- title: About me, about this site ---

Hi there! I’m Max Kreminski. I’m a design researcher in the field of computational creativity with a background (and ongoing interest) in game design. I frequently find myself designing games; programming computers; writing words; and conducting research on the subjects of human-computer interaction, creativity, and play.

In May 2017, I graduated from USC with an undergraduate degree in Interactive Media & Games. In September 2017, I’ll be starting a PhD in Computational Media at UC Santa Cruz.

Affording Play is my blog. Frequently discussed topics include:

  • Creativity-enabling tools, especially casual creators.
  • Mixed-initiative creativity: teaching computers to work alongside humans as creative collaborators and not just tools.
  • Games, interactive storytelling, and play.
  • UX design, UI design, and user research.
  • Anything else that happens to be on my mind.

You should follow me on Twitter! If you want, you can also email me; my address is just my name, as given above, at Gmail.
